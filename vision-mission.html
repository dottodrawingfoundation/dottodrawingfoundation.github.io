VISION

Dot to Drawing's core vision is to revolutionize mindsets, catalyzing alignment with contemporary needs. Our focus is on reshaping the nation's financial, social, and spiritual landscape by addressing underdeveloped thinking. We firmly believe that individual transformation of thought is the cornerstone of broader societal change. Through prioritizing 'mind over matters,' we lay the foundation for enduring stability.
We champion the dismantling of mental barriers, recognizing them as the chief impediments to progress. Esteemed figures, from entrepreneurs to saints, emphasize thoughts' world-altering potential. As Gautama Buddha wisely stated, "The mind is everything. What you think, you become." Our mission empowers individuals to overcome self-imposed limits, amplifying their impact on the world.
Dot to Drawing's universal commitment is to reveal the untapped power of thoughts. By doing so, we enable people to unlock personal and collective miracles, contributing to the betterment of humanity.
"When we speak of spirituality, we are essentially saying, 'I want to become all-inclusive or all-exclusive. I want to embrace everything as a part of myself, or I want to detach from everything, including myself.' These are the two paths – either complete inclusion or absolute exclusion. There is no in-between; it is an endless trap." ~ Sadhguru
At our NGO in Ahmedabad, we firmly believe that the essence of addressing all our challenges, big or small, lies in cultivating spiritual awareness in our society. Spiritual awareness entails not only having an inclination towards a higher power but also being deeply connected with oneself. Embracing spirituality brings forth confidence, positive energy, and abundance, essential qualities not only for personal success but also for inner contentment.
Our mission is to instill even a drop of spirituality in our students and those around us, as we know it can profoundly enhance their lives, just as it has enriched ours. When individuals discover their inner strengths and abilities, there are no limits to what they can achieve.

MISSION

Nelson Mandela once said, "Education is the most powerful weapon which you can use to change the world." Our NGO in Ahmedabad strongly believes in this philosophy and strives to provide the best education opportunities to all, regardless of their background.
Education is not just about acquiring skills for economic success; it goes beyond that. It plays a pivotal role in nation-building and fostering reconciliation among communities. At our education-focused NGO in Ahmedabad, we are committed to empowering individuals with knowledge and values that lead to a brighter future.
Our education programs cater to all age groups, emphasizing lifelong learning. We believe that education is not limited to completing various courses and degrees; it is a journey of continuous growth. By fostering qualities like patience, empathy, open-mindedness, and civility, we aim to nurture well-rounded individuals who contribute positively to society.
We firmly believe that education is the key to unlocking numerous opportunities in life. It opens doors to new possibilities and enables individuals to navigate through challenges with confidence. At our education NGO in Ahmedabad, we instill the importance of human relationships and respect from the very beginning of a child's learning journey.
Education knows no boundaries of wealth or privilege. It is a great equalizer that allows individuals to rise above their circumstances. Our NGO in Ahmedabad is dedicated to bridging the education gap for underprivileged children, offering them a chance at a brighter future.
Our approach to education goes beyond traditional academics. In addition to skill development, we focus on the multidimensional growth of each child. We offer guidance in defense skills, communication skills, business skills, and arts and music skills. By providing access to a diverse range of learning opportunities, we enable children to make informed and wise career choices. Project Art Awareness & Development
